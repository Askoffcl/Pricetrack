About PriceTrack

Revolutionizing the way you shop for electronics — faster, smarter, and locally focused.

Our Mission

PriceTrack is a web platform designed to help users compare prices for electronic devices across local offline stores. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a smart shopper, we give you the power to discover the best deals without hopping store to store.

Why PriceTrack?

  • 💡 Real-time price comparison from multiple local shops
  • 📍 Location-based shop discovery for convenience
  • 🛒 Seamless order placement and feedback system
  • 🧾 Complaint management to ensure customer satisfaction
  • 🚀 Admin tools to manage shops and ensure quality

How It Works

We aggregate pricing data from various registered retailers. Our system highlights the best deals, allows you to place orders, leave reviews, and even file complaints — all from one platform.

Future Vision

PriceTrack is evolving. We're working toward mobile app development, personalized recommendations, eco-friendly product tags, multilingual support, and even augmented reality (AR) product previews.
